Meet Ro

Hey lady, I'm Ro.

A few years ago, my life looked very different.

I was caring for my father, navigating cancer treatment, and trying to find my footing through some of life's most unexpected challenges. Somewhere along the way, I realized something I see in so many women today:

We get so busy taking care of everyone else that we forget to keep living ourselves.

That's why I do what I do.

Through wine tastings, travel experiences, meaningful conversations, and community, I create opportunities for women to reconnect with joy, confidence, connection, and themselves.

Some women find me because they want to travel.
Some come for the wine.
Some are looking for community.

What they often discover is permission to stop waiting and start embracing life again.

So take a look around.

Pour a glass, find a trip, read a reflection, or simply stay awhile.

There's a seat for you here.
